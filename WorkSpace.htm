for workspace seekers

Search
  • Select the city and workspace type you are looking for and click "search"
  • You will land on a search results page displaying all our listed workspaces under your chosen workspace type in your selected city
  • On that page, you will find a more comprehensive search & filter box to further narrow down your scope using additional criteria
  • Once you finish tailoring your advanced search criteria, press “filter” and all matching workspaces will be displayed
  • You can save this search for later reference, where you will find it stored under your user account
